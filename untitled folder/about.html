ABOUT ME

Hello and welcome!

I am Zaneta Gustyte, a beginner graphic designer and student at LCC International University. Based in Klaipada, Lithuania, I have a growing passion for creative expression and design. My journey through university has opened my eyes to the world of visual storytelling, and I am eager to learn and improve my skills along the way. As I start my career in design, I am excited to explore new techniques and styles, from minimalism to bold, dynamic creations. This website is my space to share my projects and experiences as I embark on this creative journey.
